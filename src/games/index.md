# Tank Zone — Interactive Exhibits

The Tank Zone features our most engaging installations, where visitors can observe marine life up close through reinforced viewing panes, participate in guided touch experiences, and explore aquatic ecosystems from tide pools to the deep ocean trench.

## Featured Exhibits

<section class="panel">
  <div class="panel-title">Coral Reef Corner</div>
  <div class="panel-body">
    <strong>Capacity:</strong> 40 visitors<br>
    <strong>Feeding Demos:</strong> 11:00 / 15:00 daily<br>
    <strong>Species:</strong> Clownfish, Damselfish, Blue Tang, various coral polyps<br><br>
    Step into a vibrant underwater world where tropical fish dart between living coral formations. Our twice-daily feeding demonstrations reveal the complex relationships between reef inhabitants. Children particularly enjoy watching the clownfish nestle into their anemone hosts.
  </div>
</section>

<section class="panel">
  <div class="panel-title alt">Jellyfish Room — "Moonlight Pulses"</div>
  <div class="panel-body">
    <strong>Capacity:</strong> 25 visitors<br>
    <strong>Illumination:</strong> Continuous rotation (15-min cycle)<br>
    <strong>Species:</strong> Moon Jellyfish (Aurelia aurita)<br><br>
    Soft blue lighting transforms this darkened chamber into a meditative space. Watch translucent jellyfish drift through programmed currents while ambient audio evokes deep ocean soundscapes. The "Moonlight Pulses" effect creates a rhythmic, calming atmosphere perfect for contemplation.
  </div>
</section>

<section class="panel">
  <div class="panel-title">Tide Pool Touch Deck</div>
  <div class="panel-body">
    <strong>Capacity:</strong> 15 visitors (supervised)<br>
    <strong>Hours:</strong> 10:00 – 16:00 (weekdays) / 10:00 – 18:00 (weekends)<br>
    <strong>Species:</strong> Sea stars, hermit crabs, sea urchins, barnacles<br><br>
    <strong>⚠ Gloves Mandatory</strong> — Provided at station entrance<br><br>
    Experience the texture and movement of intertidal creatures in our shallow touch pool. Staff guides demonstrate proper handling techniques to ensure both visitor safety and animal welfare. Popular with school groups and young families.
  </div>
</section>

<section class="panel">
  <div class="panel-title warning">Deep Trench Gallery — Observation Mode</div>
  <div class="panel-body">
    <strong>Capacity:</strong> 20 visitors<br>
    <strong>Audio Tour:</strong> Available via cassette (¥200 rental)<br>
    <strong>Depth Represented:</strong> 200–800 meters<br><br>
    Peer into the twilight zone through reinforced viewing panes designed to withstand extreme pressure differentials. Bioluminescent displays illuminate deep-sea species adapted to permanent darkness. Note: Certain observation windows may be temporarily obscured for maintenance or safety protocols.

## Visitor Guidelines

<section class="panel">
  <div class="panel-title">Safety & Etiquette</div>
  <div class="panel-body">
    <ul>
      <li><strong>No Tapping:</strong> Avoid tapping on glass or acrylic viewing panes—startles marine life and may trigger security alerts.</li>
      <li><strong>Photography:</strong> Flash photography prohibited in Jellyfish Room and Deep Trench Gallery.</li>
      <li><strong>Supervision:</strong> Children under 12 must remain with a guardian at all times.</li>
      <li><strong>Restricted Areas:</strong> Zones marked with red signage require staff escort or L4+ authorization.</li>
      <li><strong>Emergency Shutters:</strong> In rare cases, automated shutters may close without prior warning—please follow staff instructions calmly.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>
